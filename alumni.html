Notable Alumni

From medicine and politics to academia and journalism — meet the SFGS graduates who are shaping Kenya and the world.

Once a Daughter of St. Francis, Always a Daughter of St. Francis

"Go forth from Suwerwa with courage in your heart..."

You won't believe the number of distinguished alumnae that St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa has produced over the years — from top medical professionals and renowned journalists to successful educationists and dedicated women of faith. With such a diverse range of accomplished women emerging from SFGS, it's no wonder our school continues to be one of the most respected girls' educational institutions in Kenya.
