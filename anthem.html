School Anthem

Our Song

The voice of St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa — sung at assemblies, celebrations, and ceremonies as a reminder of who we are and where we're going.

Verse 1
St. Francis Girls we rise with faith and grace, Daughters of light, we take our rightful place, Guided by love, by prayer and honest toil, We bloom and grow on Trans-Nzoia's fertile soil, Ora et Labora, our motto rings so true, We pray, we work, for all we dare to do.
Verse 2
Our founders planted seeds of truth and care, The Franciscan spirit fills the morning air, We seek excellence in all that we pursue, In faith and learning, we are ever new, Ora et Labora, may our voices rise, As daughters strong beneath these open skies.
Verse 3
Go forth from Suwerwa with courage in your heart, In service and in love, let us do our part, St. Francis show us how to live and give, May every girl who passes through here live, Ora et Labora, we carry on your name, St. Francis Girls Suwerwa — forever our flame.
Composed by
The Sisters of St. Elizabeth, Suwerwa
