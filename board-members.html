Board of Governors

The Board of Governors (BOG) of St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa is the supreme governing body of the school, established in accordance with the Basic Education Act of Kenya. The BOG provides strategic oversight, policy direction, and governance frameworks that guide the school's operations, finances, staffing, and long-term development.

Composed of representatives from the Catholic Church, parents, the community, and professional stakeholders, the Board upholds the school's Catholic Franciscan mission and ensures that the highest standards of academic excellence, accountability, and integrity are maintained at all times. The BOG meets regularly to review the school's performance, approve budgets, and provide counsel to the School Principal and management team.
