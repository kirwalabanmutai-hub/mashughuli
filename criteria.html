Admission Criteria

Three carefully designed stages — application, aptitude test, and panel interview — to identify the future of SFGS.

★

Application · Aptitude · Interview

★

Admission to St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa is highly competitive, and prospective students must meet our set criteria to be considered. The admission process takes place in three carefully designed stages — each with its own unique requirements — beginning immediately after KCPE results are released.

Three Stages, One Goal

To identify students who will thrive academically, grow spiritually, and lead with integrity at St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa.

1
⌛ Stage One

Application & Document Review

Students submit their application forms together with required supporting documents. These are then scrutinized by the admissions office, and only those who meet the minimum academic qualifications proceed to the next stage.

Required Documents:
Completed application form
Copy of birth certificate
School transcripts / report cards
Other relevant documents
2
🧠 Stage Two

The Aptitude Test

All eligible students are invited to take an aptitude test set by the school's examination board. The test aims at evaluating the candidate's cognitive abilities, language proficiency, and mathematical skills — and is usually taken within the school's own facilities.

Areas Tested:
🧮 Cognitive Ability
📖 Language Proficiency
➕ Mathematical Skills
3
🎤 Stage Three

The Panel Interview

Shortlisted students are called for an interview with the school's admissions committee. The panel evaluates each student's life skills, character, and potential — taking into account academic achievements, extra-curricular activities, community service, and leadership qualities.

What the Panel Evaluates:
1Academic record
2Co-curricular activities
3Community service
4Leadership skills
5Character & integrity
6Personal potential

Final Decision & Notification

The school's admissions committee makes the final decision based on a combined evaluation across all three stages of the process. Successful applicants are then notified directly, and they may proceed to pay their admission fee and finalize their admission.

📅

When Does Admission Open?

The admission process at SFGS begins immediately after KCPE results are released. Stay tuned via our notice board for exact dates and deadlines each year.
