Games & Sports

Champions are made on these fields — where discipline, teamwork, and the joy of play shape the St. Francis Girls' student.

Discipline   Teamwork   Excellence

AR
Head of Department

Mr. A. Rotich

H.O.D. Games & Sports — St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa

Games and sports play a vital role in shaping the overall development of our students. From improving physical fitness to promoting social skills, there are several reasons why St. Francis Girls' prioritises athletics. Engaging in physical activities helps develop strong bones, muscles, and improves overall health — while also providing students with a valuable outlet for stress, building mental toughness and resilience.

Sports and games also promote social skills, teamwork, and communication. In team sports like netball, basketball, or volleyball, players must work together and communicate effectively to achieve their goals. Additionally, sports teach students about the importance of discipline and hard work — essential life skills that apply far beyond the playing field.

Sub-County Netball Champions

Our Girls' Netball team were crowned Sub-County Champions after a stellar performance at the Trans-Nzoia Sub-County Games — winning all their matches with outstanding teamwork and determination.

SFG Girls 42 – 10 Bidii Girls
SFG Girls 38 – 14 Lugari Girls
SFG Girls 45 – 22 Kitale Girls (Final)

A big thank you to Madam Akinyi and Mr. Rotich for coaching the team to this wonderful achievement.

Why Sports Matter at St. Francis Girls

Beyond the trophies, sports build something deeper: a sense of community and belonging. When students participate in teams and clubs, they connect to the institution, feel valued, and develop camaraderie that lasts a lifetime. Most importantly, sports give every student a chance to develop leadership skills — captains, vice-captains, and team representatives learn responsibility long before the boardroom.

Our Department Team

Apart from the coaches who are on the ground with the teams, we recognise our dedicated sports department staff for their commitment to every student's physical development and sporting excellence.
