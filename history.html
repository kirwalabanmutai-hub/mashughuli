Our History

Over a decade of academic growth and transformative education — from our founding in 2013 to today, guided by our motto, "Strive For Excellence."

Established 2013    "Strive For Excellence"

St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa is a Girls' High School located in Kitale, Trans-Nzoia County, Kenya, with a proud, growing history. Founded in 2013, the school was established with a clear mission: to provide quality education by nurturing innovativeness, responsiveness, integrity and moral Christian values in learners who will contribute positively towards national development. What began as a small institution with its first cohort of 37 KCSE candidates has grown steadily year on year.

A Journey Through Time

2013

The Founding of SFGS

St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa was established, opening with Form 1 students under founding Principal Mrs. Lunani Susan and founding B.O.M Chairperson Mr. Kimuron Ronald — with a clear vision to provide quality education to girls across the region and prepare them for higher education and life.

Foundation
2013–2015

First KCSE Cohorts

The school's first KCSE cohorts sat their examinations, establishing SFGS's academic tradition with Mean Grade C+ in each of its first three years — the school's strongest run of results to date.

Academic Foundation
2016–2022

Growth Under New Leadership

Mr. Joseph Chepkutwo took over as B.O.M Chairperson in 2016, guiding the Board through a period of steady enrolment growth — from 64 candidates in 2016 to a peak of 151 candidates in 2022.

Growth
2020–2024

Principal Hellen Zachariah

Hellen Zachariah served as Principal, and in 2023 Mrs. Peninah Komen became the school's B.O.M Chairperson, continuing the Board's oversight of the school's academic and infrastructural development.

Leadership
2024–Today

A Living Legacy

Alice K. Mutai took over as Principal in 2024. In 2025 the school posted its best mean grade since 2015 — Mean Grade C — continuing SFGS's commitment to striving for excellence.

Alumni Legacy

SFGS Today

St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa today stands as a growing institution offering a range of academic programmes and extracurricular activities including sports, music, drama, and clubs. Since its founding in 2013, the school's core commitment — to provide quality education that nurtures innovativeness, responsiveness, integrity and moral values — has never wavered.
