Who We Are

Welcome to St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa

St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa is a top-performing Girls' National Boarding School located in Kitale, Trans-Nzoia County, Kenya. Founded in 1972 by the Franciscan Sisters of St. Elizabeth, the school is well-known for its academic and co-curricular excellence, offering a vibrant, stimulating learning environment that prioritises the holistic development of students.

The school provides a rigorous academic programme that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond, with a highly qualified teaching staff who use modern techniques to make learning engaging, interactive, and memorable.

With a well-established library, well-equipped classrooms, a school chapel, and modern facilities, students at SFGS Suwerwa are assured of quality learning experiences within a Catholic Christian environment.

Our Foundation

Embodiment of Hope

St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa draws its spirit and strength from the Franciscan tradition — a tradition of prayer, joyful service, and love for all creation. Founded by the Franciscan Sisters of St. Elizabeth in 1972, the school has for over five decades embodied this spirit of prayer and dedicated service.

The exceptional performance the school has posted year after year can be attributed to dedicated, highly-trained staff, disciplined and focused students, and above all, a community that places faith at the centre of all endeavours. We give glory to God Almighty for every achievement, and we look forward with hope to the generations of women leaders yet to come.
