Notable Milestones

2013First KCSE cohort — 37 candidates, Mean Grade C+ (6.816)
2015Peak performance — Mean Grade C+ with the school's highest-ever mean score, 7.175
2020Largest cohort to date — 136 candidates
2022151 candidates — the school's largest KCSE cohort on record
2025Most recent result — Mean Grade C (5.952), the school's best mean grade since 2015

These results bear testimony to the dedication of our teaching staff, the hard work of our students, and the unwavering support of parents and the Board of Management. To God be the glory.
