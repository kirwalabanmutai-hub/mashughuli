Welcome to the SFGS Parents Zone. This page provides everything you need to stay informed and connected with your daughter's education at St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa. Access academic reports, school notices, term dates, and more — all in one place.

Zeraki Learning Portal

Access your daughter's academic results, performance reports, and progress

Zeraki Learning is the school's digital platform for academic performance tracking. Parents and guardians can log in to view exam results, class rankings, subject performance, and teacher remarks — securely and in real time. Monitor academic progress across all subjects throughout the term, and stay ahead with data-driven insights into your daughter's learning journey.

Access Zeraki Learning

Use the login credentials provided by the school at enrolment. Contact the school office if you need your access details or have forgotten your password.

Important Notice to Parents & Guardians

Parents are encouraged to maintain regular communication with the school. For academic queries, contact the Deputy Principal (Academics) during office hours (Mon–Fri, 8am–5pm). For welfare and pastoral matters, contact the Dean of Students. All school communication is official when issued through the school office or the Zeraki platform. Please disregard unofficial channels.
