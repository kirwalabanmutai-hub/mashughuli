School Policies

The framework that keeps St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa a safe, excellent, and faith-centred place to learn, live, and grow.

Faith   Excellence   Service   Integrity

Our policies exist to safeguard the values that define St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa: Faith, Excellence, Service, Integrity, and Compassion. They establish a clear, consistent framework within which every student can flourish — academically, socially, and spiritually — and every member of staff can do their best work.

Each policy listed below reflects our pursuit of excellence within a Catholic, Franciscan, girls-centred boarding environment. Policies are reviewed regularly by the school management and Board to ensure they remain current and aligned with the school's mission.

Policy Framework

Academic Policy

Standards for teaching, learning, assessment, and academic integrity across the school.

Education

Girls' Welfare Policy

Comprehensive framework for the physical, emotional, and spiritual wellbeing of every girl at SFGS.

Girls' Welfare

Code of Conduct

Expected behaviour for all students, both on and off campus, in line with our Catholic ethos.

Discipline

Boarding & Welfare

Rules and procedures governing dormitory life, student welfare, and house leadership in our girls' boarding environment.

Boarding

Dress Code for Girls

Approved school uniform and dress code for all students, including sports kit, chapel attire, and visiting days.

Uniform

ICT & Mobile Devices

Acceptable use of technology, internet access, and personal electronics on campus.

Technology

Safeguarding & Child Protection

Comprehensive safeguarding framework to protect every student's wellbeing and dignity.

Safety

Health & Medical Policy

Sanatorium procedures, medication handling, and emergency response protocols.

Health

Library & Resources

Borrowing rules, study expectations, and care of school learning materials.

Library

Chapel & Religious Life

Mass and worship attendance, religious instruction, and fostering Catholic Franciscan values.

Faith

Anti-Bullying Policy

Zero-tolerance framework for bullying, harassment, and discrimination of any kind.

Wellbeing

Home Science & Vocational Activities

Guidelines for participation in home science, cookery, and practical skills as part of holistic formation.

Vocational

Guiding Principles

  • Every policy is anchored in our Catholic Franciscan foundation and mission
  • The dignity and welfare of every girl is paramount in all decisions
  • Parents and guardians are partners in upholding the school's standards
  • Policies are reviewed regularly by the Board of Governors
  • Detailed policy documents are available from the school administration office on request

For detailed policy documents, please contact the school administration office or refer to the Student/Parent Handbook issued at admission.
