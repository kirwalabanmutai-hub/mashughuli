Our Profile

A top-performing Girls' National Boarding School rooted in Catholic Franciscan values, academic rigour, and holistic student development.

SFG
S

Motto: "ORA ET LABORA — PRAY AND WORK!"

St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa is a top-performing Girls' National Boarding School located in Kitale, Trans-Nzoia County, Kenya. The school is well-known for its academic and co-curricular excellence, offering a vibrant, stimulating learning environment that prioritises the holistic development of girls.

SFGS provides a rigorous academic programme that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond. The school has a highly qualified teaching staff who use modern teaching techniques to make learning more engaging, interactive and memorable. Whether in mathematics, science, humanities, or languages, the school's curriculum is designed to challenge students and nurture intellectual growth.

The school's emphasis on co-curricular activities enables students to develop skills and talents that complement their academic studies. With a well-established library, well-equipped classrooms, a school chapel, and modern facilities, students at St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa are assured of quality learning experiences within a Catholic Christian environment.

The school offers a supportive and faith-centred environment that encourages students to develop into well-rounded women of God. With active clubs and societies, peer mentoring, and counselling services on campus, SFGS creates a community that fosters lifelong learning. The school has a rich culture that values respect, discipline, and responsibility — all grounded in the Franciscan spirit.

We have a long-standing tradition of producing notable alumni who have made significant contributions to Kenyan society and beyond. SFGS graduates are highly respected in fields such as medicine, public service, education, journalism, and the Church. Studying at St. Francis Girls' opens doors to extensive opportunities for networking and mentorship.

What Sets Us Apart

Academic Excellence

Consistent top KCSE performance driven by qualified staff and modern teaching techniques.

Catholic Franciscan Foundation

All endeavours anchored in Catholic values, the Franciscan spirit, and a life of prayer and service.

Holistic Growth

Sports, clubs, leadership, and arts complement classroom learning in a girls-only environment.

Curriculum

In the first two years, students take a rich blend of compulsory and optional subjects designed to build a solid academic foundation. From Form Three, students sit a minimum of 7 subjects in the KCSE examination as per KNEC guidelines — most students opt for 8.

Compulsory Subjects

  • Mathematics
  • English
  • Kiswahili
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Geography
  • History
  • Christian Religious Education

Optional Subjects (choose one)

  • Agriculture
  • Business Studies
  • French
  • Computer Studies
  • Home Science
  • Music
KCSE Examination: From Form 3 onwards, students are examined on a minimum of 7 subjects and a maximum of 8 subjects as per Kenya National Examinations Council (KNEC) guidelines.

Our Location

St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa is situated in Suwerwa, Kitale in Kenya's Trans-Nzoia County. The school is well-connected to Kitale town and draws students from across Kenya as a national girls' boarding school.
