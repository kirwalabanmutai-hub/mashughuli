About Senior Secondary

St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa has embraced Kenya's Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) framework as rolled out by the Ministry of Education. Senior Secondary marks a significant milestone in a student's academic journey — it is the level at which learners make meaningful academic choices aligned with their aptitudes, interests, and career aspirations.

Grade 10 is the first year of Senior Secondary School under CBC, following the completion of Junior Secondary (Grades 7–9). At this level, students select a Learning Pathway that will guide their studies through Grades 10, 11, and 12, culminating in the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) examinations administered by the Kenya National Examinations Council (KNEC).

SFGS is fully equipped to deliver quality Senior Secondary education, with competent CBC-trained teachers, modern laboratories, a well-resourced library, and a structured pastoral support system — all within a faith-centred, girls-only environment that prioritises holistic development.

Learning Pathways

Under the CBC Senior Secondary framework, students choose one of three Learning Pathways. Each pathway is designed to align academic content with the learner's strengths and future career goals.

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics. This pathway is ideal for students interested in medicine, engineering, data science, and applied sciences. It emphasises analytical reasoning, laboratory work, and mathematical application.

Science Track

Arts & Social Sciences

This pathway covers humanities, languages, social studies, creative arts, and business. Ideal for students heading into law, journalism, public administration, education, theology, and social sciences.

Humanities Track

Technical & Applied Learning

This pathway focuses on technical, vocational, and applied skills including computing, entrepreneurship, and home science. It prepares students for technical colleges, polytechnics, and entrepreneurial careers.

Technical Track

Key Features

  • Small class sizes — ensuring individual attention and a personalised learning experience for every student.
  • Experienced CBC-trained teachers — all teaching staff have undergone competency-based curriculum training and continue professional development.
  • Modern labs and facilities — fully equipped science laboratories, a computer lab, a school library, and a chapel supporting both academic and spiritual life.
  • Co-curricular integration — academics are complemented by clubs, sports, music, drama, and community service programmes aligned with CBC's holistic philosophy.
  • Faith-centred environment — all learning is grounded in Catholic Franciscan values of prayer, service, humility, and excellence.

Entry Requirements

Admission into Grade 10 (Senior Secondary) at SFGS is open to girls who have successfully completed Grade 9 (Junior Secondary) and hold a valid KNEC Junior Secondary School Certificate. Selection is based on academic performance in the Grade 9 national assessment, pathway preference, and available places. The school is a national school and draws students from all counties across Kenya. Applicants are encouraged to apply early as spaces are limited. Please visit our Admission Overview page or the Admission Criteria page for full details.
