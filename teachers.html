Our Teachers

Dedicated, highly qualified educators committed to academic excellence and holistic student development.

SFG
S

Motto: "Strive For Excellence"

Teaching Staff

At St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa, our teaching staff are the cornerstone of academic excellence. Every teacher holds at least a Bachelor of Education degree or a professional teaching qualification recognised by the Teachers Service Commission (TSC), Kenya. Many hold postgraduate qualifications in their specialist subjects.

Our educators are passionate about empowering young women through knowledge, critical thinking, and values. They participate in continuous professional development programmes, embrace modern pedagogical techniques, and bring innovation into the classroom — ensuring every student reaches her full potential in a nurturing, faith-centred environment.

Departments

The school is organised into eight academic departments, each led by a Head of Department who oversees curriculum delivery, internal assessments, and co-curricular alignment within the subject area.

The Mathematics department nurtures logical reasoning, problem-solving skills, and numerical fluency across all forms.
