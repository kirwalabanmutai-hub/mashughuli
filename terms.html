Important Notes for Parents & Guardians

  • Students must report on the opening day of each term by 9:00 AM
  • School fees should be cleared before reporting day to avoid disruption
  • Mid-term breaks are typically 3–5 days; exact dates communicated by class teachers
  • Form 4 candidates remain in school during KCSE examination period
  • Visiting Day arrangements are announced separately each term
  • For any changes or emergencies, parents will be notified via official school channels

⚠️ Note: Dates are subject to confirmation by the Ministry of Education. Please confirm with the school administration before making travel plans.
