Mission, Vision & Values

The Catholic principles that shape every student, every classroom, every choice at St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa.

Vision   Mission   Values

Guiding Principles

At St. Francis Girls' Secondary School, Suwerwa, every decision we make and every student we form is anchored in three guiding lights: a clear Vision, a purposeful Mission, and unwavering Core Values rooted in the Catholic Franciscan tradition. Together they define who we are, what we do, and why we do it.

Our Vision

To be an excellent Catholic girls' school of choice — the institution that families, communities, and the nation trust to nurture future women leaders grounded in faith, excellence, and service.

Our Mission

To provide holistic quality education in a Catholic Christian environment guided by love, excellence, and selfless service.

Core Values

The principles we live by, daily, in the chapel and in the classroom:

Faith Excellence Service Integrity Compassion

Living Our Values

Faith is the foundation of everything at St. Francis Girls'. Rooted in the Catholic tradition and inspired by St. Francis of Assisi, every academic and co-curricular activity at SFGS is offered as an act of worship — connecting learning to God and to a life of meaning.

Excellence means striving for the highest standard — for our students, our staff, and our school. From the classroom to the sports field, we do not do anything halfway. We celebrate achievement and encourage every girl to reach her full God-given potential.

Service is at the heart of the Franciscan spirit. Every girl at SFGS is encouraged to serve — her community, her family, her nation, and the world. Leadership here is defined not by power, but by willingness to give.

Integrity and Compassion guide how we discipline, how we treat each other, and how our graduates carry themselves into society long after they leave our gates.

"In fulfilling our mission, we remain accountable to faith, excellence, and selfless service in all that we do."
